# 🛒 Amazon E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

Amazon is one of the world's leading e-commerce platforms, continuously focusing on enhancing customer experience and business performance. The objective of this project is to analyze historical e-commerce sales data, customer purchasing behavior, delivery performance, and product trends to identify opportunities for growth and customer retention.

This repository contains data cleaning processes, analytical insights, Power BI dashboards, and strategic recommendations designed to help Amazon improve customer satisfaction, optimize operations, and increase revenue.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

You are working as a Business Analyst at Amazon, a company currently performing well in the e-commerce market. Stakeholders want to maintain this growth trajectory while exploring new strategies to improve customer satisfaction and loyalty.

While Amazon already offers discounts and Prime membership benefits, the company seeks additional methods to:

* Identify and reward valuable customers.
* Improve customer shopping experiences.
* Increase customer retention.
* Reduce product returns.
* Optimize delivery performance.
* Enhance customer loyalty programs.
* Drive revenue growth through data-driven strategies.

The challenge is to leverage historical sales and customer data to uncover actionable insights and recommend business strategies that maximize customer satisfaction and profitability.

## 🔧 Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

The following data preparation steps were performed using Power Query:

### Data Quality Improvements

* Removed duplicate records.
* Eliminated blank rows and columns.
* Corrected data types for date and numeric fields.
* Handled missing values using replacements such as "NA", "Unknown Reason", and "Not Applicable".
* Removed unnecessary columns to improve model performance.

### Data Modeling

* Established relationships between Customer and Orders tables.
* Created calculated columns and measures using DAX.
* Optimized data model for reporting and dashboard performance.

## 📊 Key Insights

### Product Performance

* Phones & Tablets contribute approximately 36% of total revenue.
* Electronics contribute approximately 30.6% of total revenue.
* Fashion and Health & Beauty categories show moderate performance with growth opportunities.
* Home & Office category contributes the lowest revenue share.

### Customer Analysis

* Customer count remained relatively stable from 2015–2019.
* Significant customer growth observed in 2020.
* Indicates successful customer acquisition and market expansion.

### Top Selling Products

High-performing products include:

* Canon EOS 600D DSLR
* Canon EOS 60D DSLR
* Samsung Galaxy A02
* Amazon Fire HD 8 Kids Tablet

### Delivery Performance

Average delivery duration by delivery type:

* Express Delivery → 3.5 Days
* Standard Delivery → 10 Days
* Shipped From Abroad → 15 Days

International shipments represent the biggest opportunity for delivery optimization.

### Regional Analysis

Top Revenue Regions:

* Greater Accra
* Ashanti
* Western

Low Performing Regions:

* Amasaman
* Bono East
* Dawhenya

These regions present opportunities for targeted marketing campaigns.

### Customer Satisfaction

Analysis shows that products with ratings above 4 stars consistently generate higher sales volumes, indicating a strong relationship between customer ratings and purchasing behavior.

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

### Customer Loyalty Programs

Develop tier-based loyalty programs:

#### VIP Customers

* Early access to sales
* Exclusive discounts
* Premium support

#### Premium Customers

* Reward points
* Cashback incentives

#### Standard Customers

* Birthday rewards
* Seasonal promotions

#### Budget Customers

* High-discount campaigns
* Cashback offers

---

### Targeted Marketing

* Use customer ratings and purchase history for personalized recommendations.
* Promote high-performing products through cross-selling campaigns.
* Launch region-specific marketing initiatives.

---

### Delivery Optimization

* Partner with faster international logistics providers.
* Improve shipment tracking systems.
* Expand express delivery coverage.
* Optimize warehouse distribution based on demand patterns.

---

### Product Return Reduction

Major return reasons identified:

* Defective products
* Missing items/parts
* Product expectation mismatch
* Description mismatch

Recommended actions:

* Strengthen quality control processes.
* Improve product descriptions and images.
* Enhance packaging standards.
* Introduce stricter seller verification procedures.

---

### Inventory Optimization

* Increase stock levels in high-demand regions.
* Implement demand forecasting models.
* Improve inventory allocation across zones.
